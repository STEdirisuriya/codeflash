FAQ

What is CodeFlash?

CodeFlash is a competition organized by Ananda College ICT Society for all anandians who in grade between 6 to 11 and who are not members of ACICTS. This competiton provides different kind of tasks to do. You can select your favourite task and complete it and after you can go to next task.

Is this only for coding?

Actullay not. If you're designer you have tasks to designing. If you're like to find bugs and errors and help help us, you can do it as well. And also you have tasks for coding, programming. If you're don't know anything you can also learn and do something from that. If you want to learn, you also have tasks for that. You also can try to win.

Can I ask a question?

Yes you can, Send it to our email adress, (....) or you can add it on our chat room in dicord. (.link.) If there is a frequently ask question(FAQ), we'll add it on this page here.
